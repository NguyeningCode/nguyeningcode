Never Miss An Update

You're already busy enough with your work. Why waste time remembering every little detail of your day? Let ScrumButler help you keep track so you can spend less time recalling updates and spend more time doing what you love.

Importance Is Key

Certain things are more important than others, so why hide it? Add a blocker tag to your update to raise a flag. We'll make it highly visible to others and make sure you're heard. Because who wants to get stuck being blocked?

All In One Place

You're probably using Slack to talk with each other all the time (why else would you bother looking at a Slack bot?!) and you probably have your own set of ticket tracking system. Why not have them all in one place? With future plans on integrating with Atlassian JIRA and other ticketing systems, updating your tickets is as easy as sending out an email. Plus, with it's own tracking system that makes it easy to see what's on your plate and who's involved, you can easily have the same advantage as you get with professional ticketing softwares even if you don't have the budget for it.
